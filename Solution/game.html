Star Strike

Click to play

Objective: destroy enemy ships by shooting them. Avoid enemy missiles.
There are two control modes: flight mode and weapon mode, that you can switch between at any point.
In flight mode you have a top-down view and can move the ship, but cannot attack.
In weapon mode you control the gun turret and fire at enemies, but cannot move, leaving you vulnerable to enemy missiles.

Flight mode controls:

Thrust: W
Turn left: A
Turn right: D

Weapon mode controls:

Fire: Left click
Look: MOUSE

Switch between flight and weapon mode: SPACE

Tip: There's no friction in space. To slow down, accelerate in the opposite direction to your direction of movement.
In flight mode, the green arrow shows your current velocity.

Health: 10
